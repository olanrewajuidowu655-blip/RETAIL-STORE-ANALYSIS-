## Project Overview
    This project analyzes the performance of a retail store between 2015 and 2018. The objective is to evaluate sales performance, profitability, regional comparison, discounts, target achievements, trends, shipping modes and key influencers to uncover actionable insights that support business decision-making and future investments 
    
## Business Objectives
    - Understand the current performance of the retail store business 
    - Compare the sales performance of each region 
    - Identify opportunities to improve profitability
    - Evaluate progress towards organizational target
    - Provide data driven recommendations for future investments

## Dataset
    Source: retail store dataset 
    Period: 2015 to 2018
    Regions: West, East, South and Central
    Records include: 
    - Shipping Date
    - Shipping Mode
    - City
    - State
    - Region
    - Sales
    - Quantity
    - Profit
    - Discount
 
## Tools used
    - Microsoft Excel for Data cleaning and preparation
    - Power BI for Data modelling and visualization 

## Business Questions
    - How is the retail store performing in terms of sales and profit 
    - Which regions are performing best, and which requires improvement
    - How do discounts and shipping modes affect profitability
    - Is the business on track to achive it's sales target
    - What factors have the greatest influence on profit

## Recommendations
    - The retail store gets its highest profit from the West (37.86%) followed by the East (31.96%), these two regions represent more than 60% of the total profit, hence, they should be maintained in order to make sure they continue to generate profit for the retail store while the South and Central should be evaluated carefully to check what isn’t working, either in promotional events or demands or cost.
    
    - From the profit performance, we can expect that sales performance is also good because sales are a driver of profit, but upon looking at the profit margin in each region, the Central region should be reviewed to understand why it is generating less profit from its sales compared to other regions

    - As sales is a driver of profit, discount is a reducer of profit, the Central region has the highest discount rate across all regions at 35.77% which could explain why the profit margin is little compared to the sales so the discount should be reviewed

    - Considering the target of 4.59M, progress of 2.30M and profit of 286.40, the profit is very little compared to the sales made, the cost of production, deductions, fees and other expenses should be reviewed in order to increase the profit margin

    - The southern region, though it is the least sales generating region, its growth in sales has been consistent from 2016 to 2018, whatever has been improved in these years should be maintained while the Central on the other hand should be monitored carefully to ensure its stability is not a sign of possible decline
